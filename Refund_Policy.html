Proof of Service

To process any refund requests (if applicable), we require valid proof of booking or service engagement.

Important Note:

Do not attempt to return or redirect assigned staff directly to their source agency or home address. All communications regarding returns or replacements must be routed through our official support channels.

Partial Refunds (if applicable)

Partial refunds may be considered in specific situations, such as:

  • A service was booked but not availed due to a mutually agreed and documented reason.
  • A temporary staff member is unable to fulfill the assignment due to unforeseen circumstances, provided the issue is reported within 24 hours of service commencement.
  • Services involving additional damages or inconveniences caused not due to our error.

Time Limit for Refunds

Any refund or replacement requests must be initiated within 30 days of the service booking date. Requests submitted beyond this period will not be entertained.
